Welcome to the Battertech Super Dynamic Clock.
Resize the window however you want and the amazing dynamic clock should keep its size no matter who much you stretch and squeeze.

  1. 1 second on the clock is 0.2 seconds in real life.
  1. 1 minute on the clock is 12 seconds in real life.
  1. 1 hour on the clock is 720 seconds (12 minutes) in real life.
  1. 1 day on the clock is 17,280 seconds (4 hours and 48 minutes) in real life.

(Only tested on Firefox. If it doesn't work on Chromium-based browsers. It's your fault for using non-open source software.)
