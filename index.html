Detroit

All information and images gathered here is from the Detroit Metro Convention & Visitors Bureau's "Visit Detroit" website:
Visit Detroit

One of the biggest cities in America and the biggest city in the state of Michigan, Detroit is a hotbed of creativity and art. It once had a massive automobile industry which gave it the name Motor City. Although the auto industry sadly died, out of its corpse came inspiration. The city of Detroit might have a negative stereotype of high crime which is definitely true, I am sure the positives will far outweigh the negatives to some people.
