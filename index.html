Fullmetal Alchemist

El is one of the coolest protags I have watched. He has a tragic past in a way that doesn't feel forceful or too emo. He can go from funny to awesome to tragic seamlessly. The writers included so much emotional range with this character.

Soul Eater

One of the few female MCs in Shonen, Maka Albarn is a breath of fresh air. Some people might say that Soul is the MC, but I disagree. Most female characters in Shonen are, to be honest, blatant fan service, but Maka proved to be different and actually have a developed character.

Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagann

Simon the Digger is such an inspiring character. Watching him grow up from a scared little kid to a mature adult was a amazing journey.

Welcome to the N.H.K.

As a 32-year-old living in my mother's basement, I feel very connected to Tatsuhiro Sato. There's so many times I feel like he's me, only there is no friend to help me out.
